About Me

Hello and welcome to Ìwé Toyin! If you don't already know, the handsome man in the pictures is me - the aforementioned Toyin (or Bait to some). So, just a little introduction. I'm a German-born, Nigerian, Chemical Engineering Graduate, living in Birmingham and now working as a Senior Software Engineer specialising in Java. Ìwé Toyin loosely translates to Book of Toyin in my mothertongue Yoruba and that's what this website is. It's a collection of my thoughts and feelings in written format. Expect to find a chaotic blog, semi-useful resources I put together and some shameless self-promotion. Lastly, I painfully built and deployed this web application myself, without a website building service (I don't recommend), so if there's any general feedback, bugs or performance issues please let me know here. Thanks for reading this far and enjoy!
