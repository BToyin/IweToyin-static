About Me

Hello and welcome to Lerongba! If you don't already know, the handsome man in the pictures is me - Toyin (or Bait to some). So, just a little introduction. I'm a German born, Nigerian, Chemical Engineering Graduate, living in Birmingham and now working as a Software Engineer specialising in Java. Lerongba means "Thinking" in my native Yoruba and that's what this website is about. The website is an expression of my thinking. Expect to find a chaotic blog, semi-useful resources I put together and some shameless self-promotion. Lastly, I painfully designed this web app myself, without a website building service (I don't recommend), so if there's any feedback or small bugs please do let me know. Thanks for reading this far and enjoy!
